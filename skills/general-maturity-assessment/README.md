# General — AI Maturity Assessment

A four-part organizational AI maturity assessment using MIT CISR's four-stage model and Accenture's Foundation × Differentiation 2×2. Identifies the binding constraint and outputs a stage-advancement roadmap.

## What it does

1. Places the organization on MIT CISR's four-stage model (Experiment / Pilots / Ways of Working / Future Ready)
2. Plots against Accenture's 2×2 (Experimenter / Builder / Innovator / Achiever)
3. Frames performance implications (Stage 2 vs. Stage 3 = +14.8 pp revenue gap)
4. Produces a capability-gap roadmap to the next stage with a bold goal

## When to use it

Quarterly or before setting AI strategy. Before a board AI briefing. When scoping a multi-year AI transformation investment. When benchmarking against industry peers.

## What it outputs

- MIT CISR stage placement with evidence and NOT-YET triangulation
- Accenture archetype with binding constraint identification
- Portfolio objective-mix assessment (efficiency vs. growth vs. innovation)
- Stage-advancement roadmap with ordered capability gaps and bold goal
