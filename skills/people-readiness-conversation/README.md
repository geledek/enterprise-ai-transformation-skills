# People — AI Leadership Readiness Conversation

A four-role people and leadership gap analysis. Surfaces where CEO, manager, employee, and HR readiness gaps will block AI progress, regardless of technology quality.

## What it does

Runs structured diagnostic questions across four roles:
1. CEO: Champion vs. Cheerleader (3× McKinsey differential)
2. Manager: Role-modeling gap (88% believe it matters; 25% do it)
3. Employee: AI literacy, shadow AI risk, EU AI Act Art. 4 obligation
4. HR: Five jobs in the AI workforce transition (most orgs execute 0–2)

Outputs a prioritized 4-role gap report with specific actions and named owners.

## When to use it

Quarterly as a leadership team diagnostic. Before setting AI strategy. When AI investments aren't creating value despite good technology. Before a board or executive presentation on AI transformation.

## What it outputs

- 4-role gap report with status per role (STRONG / DEVELOPING / CRITICAL-GAP)
- Priority order with specific actions (not generic recommendations)
- Quick wins (30-day) and structural actions (60–90 day)
- The single multiplier action that unlocks the others
